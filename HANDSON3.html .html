GALLERY



The Grade 10 moving-up Ceremony of Taguig Integrated School was a proud and emotional moment. We received our certificates, listened to inspiring speeches, and looked back on our junior high journey—from challenges to fun memories with classmates. It marked the end of one chapter and the start of a new one as we prepare for senior high school. The Stand Alone Senior High School launched Brigada Eskwela 2025 with the theme “Sama-Sama Para sa Bayang Bumabasa.” Teachers, students, and volunteers united to clean and prepare the school for the new school year. The Grade 10 Taguig Integrated School prom was a magical night filled with music, dancing, and memories. Students arrived in formal attire, celebrating the end of junior high with laughter, photo sessions, and special awards. It was a night of fun, friendship, and unforgettable moments before stepping into senior high school.
Grade 9 students (2023-2024) of Taguig Integrated School danced in formal wear, performing social dances like waltz and cha-cha. It was a fun and memorable event before entering senior high. The Grade 11 First Flag Ceremony at the Stand-Alone Senior High School marked the start of the school year with prayers, the national anthem, patriotic oaths, and a message from the school head. It welcomed students into senior high and promoted unity, discipline, and school spirit. Taguig Integrated School held a UN competition where students represented different countries, discussed global issues, and showcased their skills in public speaking and diplomacy. It was a fun and educational event promoting global awareness and teamwork.
